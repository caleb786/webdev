Our History

TechPro Academy was founded in 2010 with a mission to empower individuals with cutting-edge technology skills. Over the last decade, we have grown from a small startup to a premier IT training center, offering a wide range of courses in programming, networking, data science, and more. Our graduates have gone on to secure top positions in leading tech companies worldwide.

Our Mission

At TechPro Academy, our mission is to provide high-quality, affordable education that equips our students with the skills they need to thrive in the modern tech landscape. We aim to bridge the gap between education and industry by offering hands-on, project-based learning experiences.
